About Me

Hey! I'm Joy Adhiambo, a cybersecurity enthusiast and developer based in Nairobi, Kenya. I'm transitioning from app development into the cybersecurity field with a strong focus on practical, hands-on learning.

Through CyberShujaa, I'm systematically building expertise across multiple security domains: network security, cloud infrastructure security, web application security, and penetration testing.

My approach is project-based — I learn by doing. Every HackTheBox machine, every lab, and every vulnerability I research becomes a case study on this portfolio.

I'm actively seeking opportunities to apply these skills in a professional setting and contribute to building more secure systems.
